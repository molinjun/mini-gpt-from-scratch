# 10 QA

## Slide Goal

Leave room for discussion and offer high-quality question directions.

## Key Points

- Why can a tiny model explain a large model?
- Why does GPT use next-token prediction?
- What is the core difference between attention and an ordinary MLP?
- What tradeoffs exist between character-level and production-scale tokenizers?
- How do RMSNorm/ReLU and LayerNorm/GELU differ?
- What would be the next step from a teaching model toward a practical model?

## Speaker Notes

Encourage intuition-first questions. For example: what does the model actually memorize? Can attention weights explain behavior? Will 3k names overfit? These questions all help reveal how the architecture works.
